Who is Hannah?

Filmmaker, Musician, interaction artist, whatever...

Animations, Interactions, Worlds that come to life. I see some things that you do not see and those are my dreams. Translated to anything possible. That what you see could be digital, on paper, two dimensional or even three! Through my own world, I bring you to my stories. They could be joyful, tense, adventurous and/or sad, mournful and unpleasant. That is how I am and that is how I tell. I believe people should want and have to feel everything. This is my goal. My name is Hannah and welcome to my Fantasia.
